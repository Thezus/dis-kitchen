Culinary Arts : Deserts

Fitness Nutrition : Protein Easter Eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 & 1/2 Servings = Chocolate Whey Protein
  • 1/4 Cup = Cocoa Powder
  • 1/4 Cup = Coconut Flour
  • 1/2 Cup = Milk
  • 1/2 Bar {100g Bar} = Dark Chocolate {85%}

    • Technique

    Part 1 : Easter Eggs
  • Place the whey protein,cocoa and coconut flour in a bowl.
  • Add the milk and mix untill it becomes a very thick paste.
  • Roll the mix into eggs with your hands
    {if it is too runny,add a touch of more coconut flour so that the mixture becomes dough-like} .
  • The mix should make roughly 6 eggs.
  • Melt the dark chocolate in a bain marie over a pot of boiling water.
  • Once the chocolate is melted,carefully dip the eggs into the chocolate to create a shell.

    • Part 2 : Final Touch
  • Place the chocolate-coated eggs on a baking tray
    lined with baking paper.
  • Place the eggs in a freezer until hard and then enjoy.
