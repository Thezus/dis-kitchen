Peanut Butter & Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup = Natural Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 Cup = Honey
  • 1/2 Cup = Dry Non-Fat Milk Powder
  • 1/4 Cup = Oats {Whole or Rolled}
  • 100g = Dark Chocolate {85%}
  • 1/2 Table-spoons = Kerigold Butter
  • Springkles {optional}

    • Technique

    Part 1 : Cookies
  • In a microwavable bowl, combine the peanut butter and honey.
  • Place in the microwave on high for 30-40 seconds or until the peanut butter and honey are melted.
  • Stir well and set aside.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine the milk powder and oats.
  • Pour in the peanut butter and honey mixture.
  • Mix until all the ingredients are properly combined {becomes dough-like}
  • Place the mix between two pieces of wax paper and roll out to roughly 10mm thickness.
  • Remove the wax paper and use a cookie cutter to cut out as many cookies as possible.
  • Gather scraps,reroll and re-cut.
  • Place the cookies on a baking tray and set aside.

    • Part 2 : Finishing touch
  • In a bain marie,add the dark chocolate and butter then place over a boiling pot of water.
  • Stir until the chocolate is melted.
  • Dip each coockie into the chocolate and place back on the baking tray.
  • Decorate with springkles.
  • Allow chocolate to harden and enjoy.
