Culinary Arts : Anime Themed

Japanese style : Chaliapin Steak

Ingredients

  • 2 Beef Steaks = Sirloin
  • 1 & 1/2 = Onoins
  • 2 Donburi Bowls = Rice
  • 1 Table-spoon = Umeboshi paste
  • suitable amounts of = Salt,Pepper,Potato starch
    {dissolved in water and finely chopped spring onoin
  • 4 Table-spoons = Red wine
  • 1 Tabel-spoon = Soy sauce
  • 2 Table-spoons = butter

    • Technique

    Part 1 : Tenderize Meat
  • Chop the onions finely.
  • Remove the tendons from the beef and beat it with a meat tenderizer.
  • Cover the meat with the chopped onion,
    then leave for 30 minutes.
  • Remove the onion and add salt & pepper.
    • Part 2 : Fry Onion
  • Melt 1 Table-spoon of butter in a frying pan, fry the onion used for "Part 1" until changes color.
  • Adjust the flavor with salt & pepper
  • move the onion to a dish
    • Part 3 : Frying the meat
  • Mealt the remaining butter in the frying pan from "Part 2"
  • Fry both sides of the meat before taking it out
    • Part 4 : Making the sauce
  • Add Red wine to the frying pan from "Part 3"
  • Boil it down while scrapping off the meat juice
  • Put the onion from "Part 2" back in and fry it
  • Add soy sauce fromthe pan's border and mix it with everything while adding it to season
  • Thicken it with the dissolved potato starch.
    • Final : Serve
  • Put the rice mixed with Umeboshi paste in the Donburi Bowl
  • Put the meat from "Part 3" on top of it.
  • Add plenty of the sauce from "Part 4".
  • Use the finely chopped spring onion as a topping.
    Enjoy
