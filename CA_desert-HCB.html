Hot Cross Buns

Ingredients

Icing
  • Powdered Sweatner Icing mix
    • "I suggest you use a flavour of choice,
    this can be any icing you can find of your own liking."

    ...

    Hot Cross Buns
  • Half = Cup Coconut Flour
  • 1 Third = Cup Psyllium Husk
  • 1 Tea-spoon = Baking Powder
  • 2 Table-spoons = Sweatner {Xylitol or simiar}
  • Half Tea-spoon = salt {pinch twice if you don't trust tsp }
  • Half Tea-spoon = Cinnamon
  • 4 = Eggs
  • 250ml = Boiling Water
  • Raisins or Dark Chocolate Chips {measurement is up to you}

    • Technique

    Hot Cross Buns
  • Mix all of the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  • Beat the eggs in a separate bowl until yolks and whites are combined.
  • Add the eggs and boiling water to the dry drying ingredients.
  • Mix until it forms a dough.
    • Icing
  • Mix the icing as per product instructions.
  • Use the icing to mark each hot bun with a shape of your liking {such as a cross} and allow to harden.
