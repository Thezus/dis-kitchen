Culinary Arts : Japanese Themed

Japanese style : Beef Ramenoff

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound = Beef {strips}
  • 2 Cups = Sour Cream
  • 1 Table-spoon = Chives {chopped}
  • 1 Tea-spoon = Salt
  • 1/8 Tea-spoon = Pepper
  • 1 clove = Garlic {Crushed}
  • 1/2 Cup = Grated Parmesan Cheese {divided}
  • 1 Package = Ramen noodles {any flavour}
  • 2 Table-spoon = Butter or Margarine

    • Technique

    Part 1 : Meats
  • In a Frying pan, Brown beef until done.
  • Add sour cream,spices and 1/4 cup Parmesan
  • simmer over low heat
    • Part 2 : Noodles
  • Cook noodles in water according to the package directions and drain.
  • Stir butter into warm noodles until melted
  • Fold in beef mixture.
  • Sprinkle with reserved cheese.
    Makes 2 servings.
