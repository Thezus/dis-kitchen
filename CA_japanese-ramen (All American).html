Culinary Arts : Japanese Themed

American style : All-American Ramen

Ingredients

  • 2 packages = Ramen Noodles {any flavour with seasoning packets}
  • 1/4 Cup = Chopped Onion
  • 4 Hot Dogs = {sliced}
  • 1 Cup = Grated Cheddar Cheese

    • Technique

    Part 1 : Noodles
  • Cook the noodles in water according to the package directions and drain.
  • Add seasoning packets.
    • Part 2 : Dogs
  • In a frying pan, saute onion and hot dogs together until heated through.
  • Add hot dog mixture to the noodles.
  • Add cheese and stir until melted.
    Makes 2-4 servings
